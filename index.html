About

Analyst by craft,
builder by curiosity.

I'm a Senior Data Analyst with 6+ years of experience in financial services, currently at HSBC in New York. My work sits at the intersection of credit analytics, risk management, and automation — building systems that surface the signal in noisy data.

I specialize in translating complex lending and financial datasets into clear, actionable insights through tools like Python, SQL, Alteryx, Tableau, and Google Cloud Platform. I've driven reporting automation that cut manual effort by 80% and built risk dashboards used by portfolio and relationship managers.

Outside of the day job, I'm pursuing a graduate degree in Analytics, applying Bayesian modeling, machine learning, and statistics to real-world problems across sports, finance, and customer behavior. I'm drawn to the frontier: AI agents, ML for trading, and intelligent systems that can act.
